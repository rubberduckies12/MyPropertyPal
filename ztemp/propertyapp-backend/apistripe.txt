### For your property app, you should select at least:

- `payment_intent.succeeded`  
  *(Notifies you when a payment is successful — this is essential for marking rent as paid.)*

**Optional but useful:**
- `payment_intent.payment_failed`  
  *(Notifies you if a payment fails, so you can alert the tenant or landlord.)*
- `payment_intent.canceled`
- `customer.created`
- `customer.updated`
- `account.updated` *(if using Stripe Connect for landlords)*

---

**How to select events:**
1. In your Stripe dashboard, go to **Developers → Webhooks**.
2. Click **“Add endpoint”**.
3. Enter your webhook URL (e.g., `http://localhost:5001/api/payments/webhook`).
4. Click **“Select events”**.
5. Search for and check `payment_intent.succeeded` (and any others you want).
6. Click **“Add endpoint”**.
7. After adding, click the endpoint to **reveal your webhook secret**.

---

**Summary:**  
- At minimum, select `payment_intent.succeeded`.
- Add more events as needed for your app’s features.
